Motivation: Time waits for no one

The 2020 Tokyo Olympics was postponed, obviously, every athlete will be one-year older next year. For athletes, one year could make a big difference. On this website, we are going to show you how athletes' performance correlate with ages in different sports, genders and countries, and also the trend in the modern Olympics history.

Dataset: 120-year Olympics history

The first modern summer Olympic Games was held in 1896 (the first winter Olympics was in 1924), and was held every four years since then. Our dataset contains 260k+ athletes' information (name, age, gender, nationality, medal, etc) from 1896 to 2016.

Background: sports

Some sports appear in every summer Olympic Games, e.g. athletics, swimming, fencing, etc., while some sports appear only recently, e.g. badminton (since 1992), Triathlon (since 2000). Similary, sports like Figure skating, Ice hockey and Ski jumping appear in every winter Olympics, while some sports appear only recently, e.g. short track speed skating (since 1992), snowboarding (since 1998).
